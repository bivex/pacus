# Quality Policy — pacus

**Version**: 1.0  
**Effective Date**: 2026-05-01  
**Review Date**: 2027-05-01  

## Policy Statement

pacus is committed to delivering reliable, auditable, and high-quality software for work act management and project tracking. We consistently provide products and services that meet customer requirements and comply with applicable statutory and regulatory standards.

## Commitments

### 1. Customer Focus
- Consistently deliver software that meets functional and non-functional requirements
- Ensure data integrity and traceability for all work acts and project records
- Provide clear documentation and usable HTML artifacts for printing and auditing

### 2. Conformity to Requirements
- Comply with ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System requirements
- Adhere to SQLite schema constraints and business rules encoded in triggers
- Maintain append-only audit trails for all status changes

### 3. Continual Improvement
- Regularly review and improve the Quality Management System
- Use mutation testing to verify every schema guard is necessary and effective
- Act on nonconformities through corrective actions and process improvements

### 4. Process Approach
- Integrate quality management into all development and operational processes
- Use risk-based thinking in planning and executing changes
- Ensure resources (infrastructure, competence, documented information) are available

### 5. Leadership & Accountability
- Assign clear roles and responsibilities for quality outcomes
- Ensure top management demonstrates commitment to QMS effectiveness
- Promote a culture of quality awareness and customer satisfaction

## Framework for Quality Objectives

This policy provides the framework for establishing and reviewing quality objectives at all levels of the organization, ensuring they are:
- Measurable and monitored
- Consistent with this policy
- Relevant to customer satisfaction and conformity
- Communicated and understood by all persons doing work under our control

## Communication

This policy is:
- Available as documented information in the project repository
- Communicated to all team members
- Available to relevant interested parties upon request
- Reviewed annually for continued suitability
